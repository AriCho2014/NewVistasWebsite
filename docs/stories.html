The grass area after school

I love my school so much that I don't want to go home.
I play after school in the grass area, but my parents only let me if the weather is nice.
My parents let me play for about half an hour to two hours until my parents force me to go home.
I am not the only one playing. My friends from my class and other classes also play if they can.
